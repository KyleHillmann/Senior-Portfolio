Personal Narrative

I'm passionate about technology—whether it's repairing electronics, building computers, or coding. My curiosity first sparked at the age of 13, binge-watching YouTube creators like Linus Tech Tips dissect gaming rigs and tech innovations. By 16, I talked my dad into buying an old desktop. Tinkering with upgrades, troubleshooting parts, and finally getting it to run games smoothly became my obsession. That old machine opened my eyes to how hands-on experimentation turns ideas into solutions. Today, that same drive led me to transform an aging computer into a home network managing our family's VPN and NAS—proof that old tech always has new stories to tell.
